About Call Congress

Call Congress is an initiative focused on helping progressives get organized, informed, and involved.

Formed in response to the appalling results of the 2016 presidential election, Call Congress works to help citizens contact their congresspeople. We aim to provide contact information, phone scripts, and easily understood positions. Constituents will be empowered with easier access to their representatives in government.
