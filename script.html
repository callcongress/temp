11/14/16 Trump's Cabinet

Concerning Steve Bannon's Appointment

Hi! My name is your name and I'm a constituent of your representative's name. I'm calling about representative's stance on President-Elect Trump's newest appointee, Steve Bannon. Am I speaking to the correct person?

Like I said, I'm contacting representative to express my concern over the President-Elect's appointment of Steve Bannon. As a Chief Strategist to the White House, Mr. Bannon would exert a considerable amount of influence over Mr. Trump's everyday decisions. This is severely inappropriate, since Mr. Bannon has proven himself time and time again to have extreme White Nationalist views. In fact, the Southern Poverty Law Center recently denounced him as having no business in the White House.

I'm told that Senator Harry Reid has addressed / will address the floor about this issue on Nov. 15. I strongly urge representative to join him in denouncing this dangerous and unfit man. A White Nationalist who publishes headlines like "Renegade Jew" and "Birth Control Makes Women Unattractive and Crazy" on his news website has no place advising the President. I'm sure you agree.

Thank you for your time!
